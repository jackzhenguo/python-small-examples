52 聚合迭代器
-------------

创建一个聚合了来自每个可迭代对象中的元素的迭代器：

.. code:: python

   In [1]: x = [3,2,1]
   In [2]: y = [4,5,6]
   In [3]: list(zip(y,x))
   Out[3]: [(4, 3), (5, 2), (6, 1)]

   In [4]: a = range(5)
   In [5]: b = list('abcde')
   In [6]: b
   Out[6]: ['a', 'b', 'c', 'd', 'e']
   In [7]: [str(y) + str(x) for x,y in zip(a,b)]
   Out[7]: ['a0', 'b1', 'c2', 'd3', 'e4']
